Who We Are at Open Core

"Your Phone, Your Code - Open Source Freedom. Embracing the power of open-source technology, we empower users to customize their phones freely. With transparency, security, and endless possibilities, we strive to create a community of tech enthusiasts, united by the spirit of collaboration and the pursuit of individuality."


Our Story


We started out as two people taking on the world of the mobile phone giants. We've come a long way since our initial concept and prototype. Our groundbreaking device revolutionized communication, making us global leaders. Against all odds, our journey taught us that determination and innovation can turn dreams into reality.

Our Team


Our team at Open Core are unique individuals who share a passion for open-source technology. Their diverse expertise and unwavering dedication fuel our company's success. By fostering a culture of creativity and inclusivity, we leverage our collective strengths to create revolutionary products that inspire and empower users worldwide.

Community


OpX Phone is being embraced by the open-source community as a game-changer. Its customizable features and commitment to user freedom have garnered widespread acclaim. With growing developer support and a thriving user base, OpX Phone's impact on the mobile market is undeniable, solidifying its position as a leading open-source smartphone.

History


Open Core Company was established in 2017 and since then, it has been a trailblazer in open-source solutions. With a team of passionate developers and a commitment to transparency, the company has continually expanded its offerings. Embracing community feedback, they have fostered a culture of innovation, setting new standards in the industry.
